Our Services

Data Analysis & Consultancy

TaiStat’s Data Analysis & Consultancy service helps organizations convert raw information into actionable insights. We cater to Agriculture, Healthcare, Hospitality, Retail, Finance, and more.

  • Industry Expertise: Deep experience in multiple verticals including agri-business and finance.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis: Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities using advanced statistical methods.
  • Predictive Modeling: Forecast outcomes like sales, demand, and churn with regression and time-series models.
  • Custom Dashboards: Build interactive visualizations in Power BI, Tableau, or custom web apps to monitor KPIs.
  • Capacity Building: Train teams on Python, R, SQL, and data visualization best practices.

By combining domain knowledge with technical expertise, we empower clients to make faster, data-driven decisions that lead to sustainable growth.

Get Participants & AI Taskers

Our “Get Participants & AI Taskers” service streamlines human-in-the-loop processes, providing:

  • Recruitment: Access diverse, vetted participants for surveys, user testing, and focus groups.
  • Annotation Services: Hire crowdworkers to label data, annotate images, transcribe text, and review AI outputs.
  • Quality Assurance: Consensus labeling and inter-annotator agreement to ensure high accuracy.
  • Scalability: Scale from hundreds to thousands of annotations quickly.
  • Integration: API endpoints for real-time labeling and feedback in your data pipeline.

Enhance AI model quality, reduce bias, and accelerate research initiatives by leveraging reliable human input.

AI in Schools

  • Curriculum Development: Lesson plans covering machine learning basics, neural networks, and ethical AI.
  • Hands-On Workshops: Interactive Python labs, data visualization exercises, and Kaggle-style challenges.
  • Teacher Training: Upskill educators on AI pedagogy, tools, and classroom integration best practices.
  • Competitions: School-wide AI hackathons to foster critical thinking and collaboration.
  • Career Pathways: Guidance on AI-related careers, internships, and scholarship opportunities.

Prepare the next generation for a technology-driven future by instilling AI skills and problem-solving mindsets.

Hotels & Businesses Analytics

Our “Hotels & Businesses Analytics” service provides data-driven strategies to optimize revenue and operations:

  • Dynamic Pricing: Real-time pricing models to adjust room rates and promotions.
  • Customer Segmentation: Identify high-value guests, tailor marketing campaigns, and boost loyalty ROI.
  • Operational Efficiency: Analyze POS and inventory data to reduce costs and maximize occupancy.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Mine online reviews and social media to gauge guest satisfaction and brand perception.
  • AI Chatbots: Deploy virtual assistants for bookings, check-ins, and customer inquiries to enhance guest experience.

Increase occupancy rates, foster repeat business, and deliver exceptional guest experiences with actionable insights.

Agriculture Solutions

TaiStat’s “Agriculture Solutions” empower Kenyan farmers with precise, data-driven guidance:

  • Seasonal Planning: Use historical climate and yield data to optimize planting schedules.
  • Yield Forecasting: ML models predict crop yields based on soil health, weather, and inputs.
  • Market Analysis: Identify best markets and price points using supply-demand and commodity price data.
  • Disease Detection: AI-powered image recognition identifies early signs of crop diseases and pests.
  • Precision Recommendations: Advise on fertilizer, irrigation, and pesticide application tailored to field conditions.

Maximize yields, reduce waste, and increase profitability by adopting intelligent, data-driven agricultural practices.

Climate Change Initiatives

Our “Climate Change Initiatives” support resilience and sustainability through data-driven solutions:

  • Carbon Footprint Analysis: Measure and model organizational carbon emissions across operations.
  • Risk Assessment: Use geospatial and climate projection data to identify vulnerable areas.
  • Adaptive Strategies: Develop plans for water management, reforestation, and disaster risk reduction.
  • Renewable Feasibility: Analyze solar, wind, and biogas potential based on local climatic data.
  • ESG Reporting: Generate sustainability reports aligned with GRI and SASB frameworks.

Promote ecological resilience and long-term environmental health with strategic, data-backed initiatives.
