Who We Are

Mission and Vision

At TaiStat, our mission is to empower businesses, institutions, and individuals by delivering innovative, data-driven solutions while fostering data literacy across all levels. By leveraging a network of real, engaged participants and AI taskers, we deliver high-quality data to support research, train and evaluate AI models, and seamlessly integrate human feedback into client workflows. We provide expert data analysis and AI-driven support in key sectors such as agriculture, health, hospitality, education, and business helping clients make smarter decisions, improve efficiency, and achieve sustainable growth. Our vision is to become the leading Data Consultancy Firm in Kenya and the East Africa region, championing innovation and excellence in Artificial Intelligence.

We are committed to helping you unlock the power of data and insights tailored to your unique challenges. By transforming knowledge into practical solutions, we aim to drive real-world impact and sustainable success. At TaiStat, excellence is not just a goal—it is at the core of everything we do as we bridge the gap between data and decision-making.
