Meet the Founder

Stephen Mulingwa

Stephen Mulingwa is a visionary Data Scientist, Machine Learning expert, and AI strategist, dedicated to unlocking the transformative power of data. As the Founder of TaiStat Consultancy, he leads a firm committed to delivering AI, data science, data consultancy, training and tutoring, and analytics solutions that drive innovation, efficiency, and growth across industries worldwide.

With deep expertise in machine learning, predictive analytics, and AI-driven decision-making, Stephen has successfully designed and deployed advanced models that solve complex business challenges ranging from customer behavior prediction and operational forecasting to healthcare analytics and risk assessment. His data-driven solutions empower organizations to make informed, strategic decisions, enhancing productivity and long-term sustainability.

Beyond consulting, Stephen is deeply committed to education, mentorship, and knowledge sharing. He has trained and guided professionals, businesses, and aspiring data practitioners in mastering data analytics, machine learning, and AI applications, fostering a new generation of data-driven leaders.
