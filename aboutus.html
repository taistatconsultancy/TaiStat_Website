About Us

TaiStat Firm is a technology‑driven company located in the beautiful suburbs of Nairobi City, Kenya, built to serve the needs of today’s data‑focused and AI‑powered world. We specialize in providing smart, scalable solutions in artificial intelligence, data collection and analysis, machine learning support, automation, survey execution, user research, web development, and digital development.

Our team is made up of highly skilled professionals who have worked extensively in the fields of AI, data science, automation, and digital transformation. Whether it’s annotating datasets for machine learning, developing WhatsApp chatbots, running large‑scale surveys, or creating dynamic, responsive websites, we deliver work that is precise, timely, and aligned with global standards.

TaiStat supports a wide range of industries, including Education, Artificial Intelligence, Research, Hotels, E‑commerce, Agriculture, and Technology. Our services are trusted by clients looking to scale their projects, automate operations, or gain accurate insights from data in emerging markets.

Choosing TaiStat as your partner, you're not just outsourcing a task—you’re gaining a committed team that collaborates with you every step of the way. We take the time to understand your objectives and tailor our approach to ensure your goals are met with excellence.

Smart solutions. Real impact. TaiStat delivers.

Meet the Founder

Stephen Mulingwa

Stephen Mulingwa leads TaiStat’s strategic direction and AI innovation. As the visionary behind the firm, he plays a central role in guiding the company’s mission to provide intelligent, scalable, and ethical data solutions. His expertise lies in AI training for research, where he helps institutions build smart, data-driven systems for academic studies, social impact research, and market intelligence.

Stephen has led numerous projects involving predictive modeling, decision support systems, and healthcare analytics, making AI both accessible and impactful for real-world applications. At TaiStat, his leadership extends beyond technical work—he cultivates partnerships, mentors talent, and ensures each engagement delivers measurable value.

His commitment to empowering teams and clients through AI-driven research tools sets TaiStat apart as a forward-thinking, trusted partner in the global data ecosystem. Stephen has trained and guided professionals, businesses, and aspiring data practitioners in mastering data analytics, machine learning, and AI applications, fostering a new generation of data-driven leaders.
